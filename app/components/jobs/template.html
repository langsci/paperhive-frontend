Join the PaperHive team

PaperHive is a software company in the field of scientific research and collaboration. After becoming a part of the Centre for Entrepreneurship incubator at the TU Berlin in mid 2015 and receiving the EXIST startup grant since December 2015, the company is releasing its product in Q2 2016.

PaperHive is the coworking hub for researchers, aiming to support knowledge-creators in their work and to dramatically increase open collaboration in science.

We strive for high diversity in our team and encourage you to apply regardless of your (and our) identity.

We are always looking for an incredibly creative developer willing to take over responsibility for engineering our existing application in a user-friendly and sustainable way.
