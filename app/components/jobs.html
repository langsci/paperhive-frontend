Join the PaperHive team

PaperHive is a software company in the field of scientific research and collaboration. After becoming a part of the Centre for Entrepreneurship incubator at the TU Berlin in mid 2015 and receiving the EXIST startup grant since December 2015, the company released its first product as public beta in June 2016.

PaperHive is the coworking hub for researchers, aiming to support knowledge-creators in their work and to dramatically increase open collaboration in science.

We strive for high diversity in our team and encourage you to apply regardless of your (and our) identity.

Software engineer backend and/or frontend full-time

We are looking for an incredibly creative developer willing to take over responsibility for engineering our existing application in a user-friendly and sustainable way.

Your profile

We need a team player to join PaperHive in September 2016 who
  • has a bachelor's or master's degree in computer science (or a related field)
  • has ≥1.5 years of development experience with Node.js and/or AngularJS
  • wants to stay at the forefront of web technology by learning and applying new concepts every day
  • loves to build reusable code with accompanying unit tests
  • makes decisions based on facts instead of buzzwords
  • speaks English or German fluently

Experience with large-scale web applications, TypeScript, ES6 or Angular 2 is a plus.
