Let high-quality academic literature stand out

PaperHive is a cross-publisher layer of interaction on top of research documents. The platform embeds communication in the researcher's reading process and workflow and helps high-quality literature stand out.

Easy and fast integration

Integration with PaperHive is easy and fast: only basic article meta data from the publisher is required. Contact us for more information!

Access rights

All article traffic to the documents generated thanks to PaperHive goes to the publisher servers. Only users with the proper access rights can view subscription-based content covered under their institutional licence.

Annotation standards

PaperHive follows W3C Web Annotation standards and is a part of the Annoating All Knowledge Coalition. Discussions are safely archived with trusted preservation service providers.
